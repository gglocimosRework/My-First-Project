A Museum fully opens in gala ceremony

Egypt’s Grand Museum fully opens in gala ceremony after 2-decade build, Egypt on Saturday held a glittering ceremony attended by European and Arab royals and presidents, it was one of the country's most ambitious cultural projects -- after several years of delay.

When and How

The project was hit by financial crises, Egyptian Prime Minister Mostafa Madbouli speaks to the press prior to the official opening of the Grand Egyptian Museum in Giza, on the southwestern outskirts of the Egyptian capital Cairo on November, 1, 2025

After decades of construction and delays. It has finally opened! It houses around 50,000 artifacts, including the entire Tutankhamun collection, in a modern, sustainable facility. Key highlights include the Ramses II statue, grand staircases, and panoramic views of the pyramids. The GEM marks a major cultural and tourism milestone for Egypt.

Fun Facts:

You can find it below:↓

Fact 1

At the museum’s entrance stands a massive 11-meter-tall statue of Ramses II, one of the largest free-standing statues ever moved from its original location.

Fact 2

The museum’s design includes a six-story grand staircase lined with statues, and from the upper galleries, visitors can see breathtaking panoramic views of the Giza pyramids

Fact 3

The museum includes a dedicated conservation center, where visitors can watch experts restore and preserve ancient artifacts using advanced techniques, making it a living showcase of archaeology.
